Frequently Asked Questions

Why is there no activity today?

DayBloom is a weekday app — Saturday and Sunday are rest days with no scheduled activity. If it's a weekday and nothing appears, try pulling down to refresh.

How do I change my child's age range?

Tap your child's avatar in the top-right corner of the Today screen to open their profile, then update the age range. DayBloom will also prompt you automatically when your child reaches a new milestone age.

Can I swap today's activity for a different one?

Yes — tap "Different activity today?" below the activity card. You get one swap per week.

How does difficulty work?

Activities are rated Easy, Medium, or Challenging. DayBloom tracks your pace feedback ("Too easy", "Just right", "Too hard") and automatically adjusts the difficulty level over time.

How do I delete my account and data?

Go to Profile → Delete account & all data. This permanently deletes your account, your child's profile, and all activity history. It cannot be undone.

Is my child's data private?

Yes. We store only what's needed to run the app (child's name, date of birth, and activity history). We never sell or share your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Something else?

If your question isn't covered above, email us at support@daybloom.app and we'll get back to you.
